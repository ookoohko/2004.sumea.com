JOBS

Game Tester

Are you interested in mobile games and playing future hit games? This is THE place for you! Sumea is now looking for part-time and full-time Game Testers to its Quality Assurance Team.

A game tester will play test games and seek technical errors and inconsistencies in game play and report these issues to producers and development teams.

Most important qualifications for this position are:
Enthusiastic attitude towards mobile games and game testing
Patience to test games, follow test procedures and report issues accurately
Positive attitude and ability to communicate and cooperate with other people

Sumea requires that testers are available for work on short notice. Testing is conducted at Sumea’s office in Helsinki and therefore only applicants living in Helsinki area should apply.

Interested applicants are asked to submit their application and a resume via email to careers@sumea.com. Please use “GAME TESTER” as a subject of the email. Applications are accepted until 6.8.2004.


About Sumea
Sumea Interactive Ltd. is an award-winning game publisher and developer that creates high quality, innovative, and technically-superior games for mobile phones. Sumea's games are available around the world, including through Vodafone, O2, T-Mobile, Orange, AT&T Wireless, Cingular Wireless, Verizon Wireless, Wind, Telefonica, Swisscom, Mobilkom, TeliaSonera, Jamba! and Nokia Shops. Founded in 1999, Sumea is headquartered in Helsinki, Finland and is the European headquarters of its parent company, Digital Chocolate, Inc., Digital Chocolate is a developer of innovative mobile applications for life, work, and play. Based in San Mateo, California and founded in 2003 by software gaming pioneer Trip Hawkins, Digital Chocolate is backed by Kleiner Perkins Caufield & Byers, and Sequoia Capital.
