RACING FEVER 2

 
 

It's race time! All of the most furious mad caps are in town, so let's rock 'n' roll! Racing Fever 2 is the official sequel to Sumea's most famous game to date, which by now can be considered as the benchmark for mobile racing games.

The game features multiple tracks with different weather conditions. Racing commences when the sun is setting, continues through the rainy night and ends at dawn.

The objective in Racing Fever 2 is to drive better and faster than your current opponent to unlock the next track. Collect power-ups to overtake slower cars and show the hardest drivers who’s the new boss in town! If you find ordinary overtaking too easy, you can always find your way to the ramps and jump over obstacles or other cars.

Story
Sam has just moved to a town which is known as the heaven of underground street racers. Sam wants to be as famous as his father, a legendary track racer, but his welcome wasn’t too warm as nobody really believed the new guy would have any real chance in the races. To prove his driving skills, Sam joined a competition which he easily won. The race was just for amateur drivers but now he has to face the real challenge as the hardest drivers in town want to test him.

In addition to all that, there is a rumour about an unbeatable driver who only drives against the best of the best — maybe some day Sam will discover the secret of this unknown driver…
