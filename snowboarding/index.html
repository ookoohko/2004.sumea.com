EXTREME AIR SNOWBOARDING™

 
 

It's time to hit the pipe and powder some snow again! Pull off as many tricks as possible to impress the judges and accomplish the missions in Career Mode. If you are unsure how to complete some of the fearsome tricks you should try Tutorial Mode and follow your trainer’s instructions to become a true snowboarder.

Extreme Air Snowboarding™ is an extreme winter sports game with smooth 3D graphics. The game features a healthy amount of modes and, in addition to performing tricks, you get to ride the pipe as fast as possible trying to beat your trainer’s time or, if you prefer technique over speed, you can try to collect more items than your personal trainer in an attempt to show him who the best boarder really is! There is also the possibility to compete against your friends in hot-seat Multiplayer Mode.

To perform tricks in Career Mode, you jump in the air. Collect power-ups or run over speed arrows to increase your speed and jump even higher. Use rails and boxes on the flat bottom to perform trick combos. To score maximum points, get familiar with the trick combo system, which can increase your score greatly! But be careful… You’ll be eating snow if you try to perform tricks that are too complicated and can’t land your board. Last but not least, judges will give you extra points for impression… If your performance is worth it!

Playability has been polished to meet the expectations of sports and snowboarding fans, and provides an enjoyable gaming experience time-and-time again. Career Mode includes 11 missions and even if only true extreme sports players can accomplish all of the missions, there is still plenty of game-play for rookies.

Game graphics are pure Sumea quality. Each of the modes has a unique beautiful background and the animation of the snowboarder is as smooth as silk.

Now it’s all up to you… Jump into the icy half pipe and show us what you got!
