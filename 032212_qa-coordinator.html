JOBS

QA Coordinator

Are you an organized and quality-oriented professional? Interested in Mobile Games? Then we’ve got a challenging position for you! Sumea is looking for a full-time Quality Assurance Coordinator whose primary responsibilities will be:

Managing both internal and external quality assurance personnel.
Planning and implementation of QA processes and practices.
Consulting game development and integration of quality assurance activities to the development process.

The successful candidate will have completed at least a Bachelor’s level degree in computer science or related subject. Work experience in software testing and quality assurance is required. All work experience in game testing, mobile gaming and Java/J2ME is considered an advantage.

Personal qualities and professional skills that are important in the position include:

Strong organization skills and fundamental commitment to quality.
Ability to work in a team environment and good communication skills.
Knowledge of testing, issue tracking and release management practices and tools.
Attention to detail.
Ability to cope with rapidly evolving business and technology environment.
A lot of interest and also some knowledge of games and games industry.

In return Sumea will provide an exciting and creative atmosphere where ideas of talented individuals are always appreciated. Sumea is developing fast, which enables capable people to grow alongside the company.

Interested applicants are asked to submit their application and a resume via email to careers@sumea.com. Applications will be accepted until 15.1. 2004. Ville Haaramo may be contacted for further information about this vacancy by email or phone: ville@sumea.com, Mobile +358 50 3206942.


Sumea is a game development and publishing company that was founded in 1999. An award-winning company that focuses on creating high quality, innovative and technically superior games for Java-enabled mobile phones. Sumea’s products are currently available in 40 countries worldwide by telecom operators, mobile entertainment portals and handset manufacturers. Customers include industry leaders Vodafone, Orange, T-Mobile, AT&T Wireless and Nokia.
