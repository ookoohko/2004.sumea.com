JOBS

Game Producer

Can you lead teams of designers, artists and programmers? Then we’ve got an exciting position for you! Sumea is looking for a full-time Game Producer for its Game Development Team. To be the perfect candidate, you:

are very thorough and can handle a lot of detail
have working experience with Microsoft Project, Excel and Word
communicate very clearly both in speech and in writing, in English
speak and write passable Finnish, and understand spoken Finnish
are able to understand the concerns and issues of programmers, artists and game designers
can say NO to people, with courtesy of course
distribute work evenly
inspire people to work as a team
love games!
experience in branding and positioning of products is a plus

In return Sumea will provide a friendly, productive atmosphere where ideas of talented individuals are always appreciated. At Sumea, we want to make sure you have all the necessary tools, and the support of an ever-growing organization to produce great games with enthusiastic, creative people. Sumea is developing fast, which enables capable people to quickly grow into more senior roles.

Interested applicants are asked to submit their application and a resume via email to careers@sumea.com. Please write “PRODUCER” on the subject line. Applications will be accepted until 31.3.2004. Lasse Seppänen may be contacted for further information about this vacancy by phone: +358 40 749 8003.

In total, Sumea's games are being distributed in 45+ countries. For more information, see www.sumea.com
