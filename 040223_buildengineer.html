JOBS

Build Engineer

Interested in mobile games? Then we’ve got a challenging position for you! Sumea is looking for a part-time or full-time Build Engineer. Primary responsibilities in this position are:

Porting and localizing games to different technologies, handsets and languages
Product configuration management and configuration tools development
Quality assurance

The successful candidate is an advanced student or recent graduate in Bachelor’s or Master’s degree programme in computer science or related subject. All work experience in Java/J2ME software development is considered an advantage. Build Engineer position is an entry-level position.

Personal qualities and skills that are important in the position include:

Attention to detail and meticulous and organized working habits.
Skill and experience in software engineering, especially with Java and J2ME.
Knowledge of configuration management and quality assurance practices.
A lot of interest and also some knowledge of games and games industry.

In return Sumea will provide flexible working hours in an exciting and creative atmosphere where ideas of talented individuals are always appreciated. Sumea is developing fast, which enables capable people to grow alongside the company.

Interested applicants are asked to submit their application and a resume via email to careers@sumea.com. Please use “Build Engineer” as a subject of the email. Applications that clearly indicate desired monthly workload (minimum 75 hrs) and a salary requirement are given priority. Applications are accepted until 5.3.2004.

In total, Sumea's games are being distributed in 45+ countries. For more information, see www.sumea.com
