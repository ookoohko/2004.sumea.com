FAQ

Q. Can I buy games from sumea.com?
A. No, that's not possible yet. Instead go to the ‘Where to buy’ section to find out who distributes our games.

Q. What kind of phone do I need to play the games?
A. Most of our games work on java-enabled colour screen mobile phones, but it depends on the game. Check the information from your mobile service provider.

Q. How much do these games cost?
A. The usual download price is 3—5 euro.

Q. Does Sumea publish games developed by external studios?
A. Yes we do. If you have a game that you would like to offer to Sumea, email information about your company, the title you are offering and background of the developers. We'll contact you if we need to know more in order to proceed.

Q. Is Sumea using external studios to produce games?
A. Yes, we use external studios. Please send information about your company, games you have already published and background of the key personnel to developer@sumea.com.
