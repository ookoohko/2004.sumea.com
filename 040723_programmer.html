JOBS

Game Programmer

Can you write solid code for games? Then we’ve got an exciting position for you! Sumea is looking for a full-time Game Programmer for its Game Development Team. To be the perfect candidate, you:

Have experience in graphics-intensive programming and / or game development
Have good teamwork skills
Have at least basic knowledge of 3D programming
May have education in computer science and graphics
Have sample games or demos, preferably running on a mobile phone or emulator
Love games!

In return Sumea will provide a friendly, productive atmosphere where ideas of talented individuals are always appreciated. At Sumea, we want to make sure you have all the necessary tools, and the support of an ever-growing organization to produce great games with enthusiastic, creative people. Sumea is developing fast, which enables capable people to quickly grow into more senior roles.

Interested applicants are asked to submit their application and a resume via email to careers@sumea.com. Please write “PROGRAMMER” on the subject line. Applications will be accepted until 15.8.2004. Lassi Leppinen may be contacted for further information about this vacancy by phone: +358 40 550 1178.


About Sumea
Sumea Interactive Ltd. is an award-winning game publisher and developer that creates high quality, innovative, and technically-superior games for mobile phones. Sumea's games are available around the world, including through Vodafone, O2, T-Mobile, Orange, AT&T Wireless, Cingular Wireless, Verizon Wireless, Wind, Telefonica, Swisscom, Mobilkom, TeliaSonera, Jamba! and Nokia Shops. Founded in 1999, Sumea is headquartered in Helsinki, Finland and is the European headquarters of its parent company, Digital Chocolate, Inc., Digital Chocolate is a developer of innovative mobile applications for life, work, and play. Based in San Mateo, California and founded in 2003 by software gaming pioneer Trip Hawkins, Digital Chocolate is backed by Kleiner Perkins Caufield & Byers, and Sequoia Capital.
