SURVIVOR®

 
 

Be the ultimate Survivor®!

Join other castaways on a tropical island in this mobile game of the world-famous Survivor® TV-show. Catch fish to earn the respect of your tribe, compete in various challenges to gain immunity against being voted off the island, or form alliances with fellow castaways to ensure that the vote will be in your favour. You can also play the challenges as separate games with your friends using the hot-seat multiplayer option.

The official Survivor® game with its unique look and feel brings all the excitement of Castaway’s international TV hit to mobile gamers. Great graphics, exciting music and easy but challenging gameplay recreate the original Survivor® atmosphere — an irresistible gaming experience to enjoy anywhere at any time. The adventure is about to begin! The game features six great mini-games and two game modes. In the Career Mode the player experiences the adventure just like in the television series — from daily life with immunity challenges to thrilling Tribal Council meetings. The game starts as the player selects one of the final 5 castaways. Life in a tropical paradise isn’t easy. Food is essential and fishing is the way to feed the tribe. A good catch ensures popularity among the others, but a bad one makes life difficult. Later it’s time for an immunity challenge and victory ensures that the player won’t be voted off the island. In the evenings, tribe gathers to Tribal Council to vote. One castaway will be voted off from the island. Once the competition is narrowed down to two finalists, it is time for the final challenge. And at last there is only one player left — the Ultimate Survivor®!

In the Single Challenge mode the player selects one of the mini-games to play. The versatile mini-games challenge the player’s courage, strength, speed, balance, concentration and memory. Success in mini-games requires skill, but they are snap to learn. Single Challenge mode allows up to five players to enjoy the excitement of the game with a single mobile phone. It is the perfect place to practice for the Survivor® challenge.

Have you ever wanted to be in the Survivor® TV-series? Now this game gives you the chance. Be a castaway. Be the Ultimate Survivor®! Sumea’s exceptional game ensures hours of fun and excitement, larger-than-life emotions —– alone or with friends. It’s all about surviving — Are you ready for the adventure?
