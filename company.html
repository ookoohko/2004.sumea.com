Sumea is a game development and publishing company specialising in downloadable mobile games. Many of the games are already living mobile classics.

The company was founded in 1999 and focuses on creating high quality, innovative and technically superior games for J2ME and BREW platforms. Sumea games combine vivid graphics with extraordinary game-flow. The games are hard to master but at the same time very intuitive to play.

Sumea understands the nature of different gaming platforms. The company’s mission is to offer instant fun for everyone — meaning a wide array of downloadable mobile games available on multiple technology platforms. Sumea supports both J2ME and BREW technologies and a wide range of mobile devices from various handset manufacturers.

For its global customers Sumea offers more than just games. Market know-how and extensive marketing materials, flexibility and down-to-earth, no-nonsense attitude are benefits that most of Sumea's customers value. When the customer looks good, the company looks good is the philosophy.

Sumea is based in Helsinki, Finland. Sumea people share a real passion for quality gaming. Sumea aims to foster its position as a company that sets industry standards.
