JOBS

Operations Engineer — Games

Interested in Mobile Games? Then we’ve got a challenging position for you! Sumea is looking for a full-time Operations Engineer for its Operations Team. Primary responsibilities in this position are:

Porting and localizing games to different handsets and languages
Implementation of customer-specific features to Sumea games
Technical contact point for international distributors
Delivery of games and materials to distributors

Daily tasks will also contain implementation of configuration management tools and quality assurance activities.

The successful candidate will have completed, or is close to the completion of, at least a Bachelor’s level degree in computer science or related subject. All work experience in Java/J2ME software development, mobile gaming and interacting with international customers is considered an advantage.

Personal qualities and skills that are important in the position include:

Outspoken individual possessing good communication skills, especially in English and Finnish.
Attention to detail.
Have the will and patience to find solutions to issues presented by customers
Skill and experience in software engineering, especially with Java and J2ME.
A lot of interest and also some knowledge of games and game development.

In return Sumea will provide an exciting and creative atmosphere where ideas of talented individuals are always appreciated. Sumea is developing fast, which enables capable people to grow alongside the company.

Interested applicants are asked to submit their application and a resume via email to careers@sumea.com. Applications will be accepted until August 15, 2003. Mr. Ville Haaramo may be contacted for further information about this vacancy by email or phone: ville@sumea.com, Mobile +358 50 3206942.

Sumea is a game development and publishing company that was founded in 1999. An award-winning company that focuses on creating high quality, innovative and technically superior games for Java-enabled mobile phones. Sumea’s products are currently available in 37 countries worldwide by telecom operators, mobile entertainment portals and handset manufacturers. Customers include industry leaders Vodafone, Orange, T-Mobile, AT&T Wireless and Nokia.
