JOBS

Account Manager — Asia-Pasific

Interested in international business? Then we’ve got a challenging position for you! Sumea is looking for a full-time Account Manager for its Sales & Account Management Team for the Asia-Pacific area.

Primary responsibilities:

Day-to-day management of a selected number of distribution channels i.e. customer accounts in the APAC market.
Achieving high visibility for new products in the channel by planning, negotiating and implementing promotions and marketing campaigns
Liaising with all areas of the business in order to assure the highest level of customer satisfaction
Monitoring sales and identifying new business opportunities
Key competitor analysis

Professional skills:

The successful candidate will have a Bachelor’s level degree in Business Administration, in Economics or in Polytechnics
All work experience in telecom and/or games industry will be an asset

Personal qualities:

Excellent knowledge of English language
Additional language skills are highly valued
Enjoys working in a fast-paced, dynamic environment with continuous challenges
A flexible thinker who generates new ideas and business solutions
A lot of interest and maybe some knowledge of games and game development.

Along with competitive salary and benefits package Digital Chocolate will provide an exciting and creative atmosphere where ideas of talented individuals are always appreciated. Digital Chocolate is growing rapidly, which enables capable people to grow alongside the company.

Please mail your application to careers@sumea.com by 24th of October. Please indicate your application with “Account Manager — APAC” in the subject field. For further details, please contact Ilkka Paananen p. +358 40 594 8173.


About Digital Chocolate
Digital Chocolate develops entertainment, problem-solving, and social networking software for mobile phones, and publishes games under the Sumea brand. The company is based in San Mateo, California with its European headquarters in Helsinki, Finland. Founded in 2003 by software gaming pioneer Trip Hawkins, Digital Chocolate is backed by Kleiner Perkins Caufield & Byers, Sequoia Capital, Sutter Hill Ventures, Chengwei Ventures and angel investors including Bob Pittman, former COO of AOL/Time-Warner and founder of MTV.

The Sumea line of award-winning mobile games is available through a wide network of distinguished partners all over the world, including Vodafone, O2, T-Mobile, Orange, AT&T Wireless, Cingular Wireless, Verizon Wireless, Wind, Telefonica, Swisscom, Mobilkom, TeliaSonera, Jamba! and Nokia Shops.
