JUMBO RUMBLE™

 
 

Sumea invites you to Jumbo Rumble™ where up to three players can compete simultaneously on a single handset. You can hone your elephant guiding skills in the melon-smashing challenge mode, or go head-to-head against your friends or computer-controlled opponents in two multiplayer events, knocking elephants overboard and dodging the pesky fly. Learning is easy — you only need one button to play!

Pocket League: Jumbo Rumble™ features three different gaming modes, all carried out in the same laid back style. Action takes place on a high platform, where the player and/or computer controlled elephants rumble. The single player tries to satisfy her elephantic hunger by smashing the here and there appearing melons. The multiplayer may choose to cause trouble to her mates either by tackling them over the edge or by infecting them with an itchy insect. Up to three players may play on a single handset. Possible lack of friends may be compensated with computer driven elephants.

So unleash the big animal in you and trap it into your mobile phone! Get your thick and hairy skin insect bitten and covered with melonshells! Push your fellow beasts over the edge! Prevail and be the elephant of the platform!
