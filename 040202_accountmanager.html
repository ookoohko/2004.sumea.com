JOBS

Account Manager

Interested in international business? Then we’ve got a challenging position for you! Sumea is looking for a full-time Account Manager for its Sales & Account Management Team. Primary responsibilities in this position are:
Day-to-day management of a selected number of distribution channels i.e. customer accounts.
Maximizing the revenue stream from the designated customer accounts by participating in marketing planning, managing new product launches to the channels and creating new business opportunities.

The successful candidate will have completed, or is close to the completion of, at least a Bachelor’s level degree in Business Administration, Economics or Polytechnics. All work experience in telecom and/or games industry is considered an advantage, as is all previous experience from managing customer relationships.

Personal qualities and skills that are important in the position include:
Good communication skills, especially in English and Finnish.
Additional language skills in French, Spanish and/or Italian are highly valued
Ability to manage multiple simultaneous tasks effectively.
A lot of interest and also some knowledge of games and game development.

In return Sumea will provide an exciting and creative atmosphere where ideas of talented individuals are always appreciated. Sumea is growing rapidly, which enables capable people to grow alongside the company.

Interested applicants are asked to submit their application and a resume via email to careers@sumea.com. Applications will be accepted until 1st March 2004.


Sumea is an award-winning game publisher that was founded in 1999. Sumea focuses on creating and distributing high quality, innovative and technically superior games for mobile phones. Today, Sumea's games are available through the best distribution channels all over the world, including Vodafone, O2, T-Mobile, Orange, AT&T Wireless, Cingular Wireless, Verizon Wireless, Wind, Telefonica, Swisscom, Mobilkom, TeliaSonera, Jamba! and Nokia Shops. In total, Sumea's games are being distributed in 45+ countries. For more information, see www.sumea.com
