JOBS

Game Artist

Can you create beautiful game art for mobile phones? Then we’ve got an exciting position for you! Sumea is looking for a full-time Game Artist for its Game Development Team. We are looking for the following:

2D pixel graphics and sprite animations skills (samples required)
Low polygon 3D modelling, animation and texture mapping skills
Ability to create exciting, unique game characters
Traditional art training and drawing skills
Graphic design and typography skills
Attention to detail
Ability to deliver work within the agreed schedule
Photoshop and 3D Studio Max experience
Level design experience is a plus but not required
Personal interest in games is a must!

In return Sumea will provide an exciting and creative atmosphere where ideas of talented individuals are always appreciated. At Sumea, we want to make sure you have all the necessary tools, and reasonable time to produce great game art. Sumea is developing fast, which enables capable people to grow into more senior game artist roles.

Interested applicants are asked to submit their application and a resume via email to careers@sumea.com. Please write “ARTIST” on the subject line. Applications will be accepted until 13.2.2004. Lasse Seppänen may be contacted for further information about this vacancy by email or phone: lasse@sumea.com, mobile +358 40 749 8003.


Sumea is an award-winning game publisher that was founded in 1999. Sumea focuses on creating high quality, innovative and technically superior games for mobile phones.
